Speaks Album - recognize text from your photos and speaks it right away.

Speaks Album is a tool to recognize the text from your photos and speaks it right away.
It's recommended that you have iOS 16 version that to be recognized various languages.

Main View
* Camera Icon - to use the photos from your photo album.

Image View
* [ Edit area ] - edit the target area. you can drag and zoom the image.
* Buttons
  - [ recognize ] - recognize the text from the image.
  - [ speak ] - speak the recognized text.
  - [ pause/resume ] - pause/resume speaking.
  - [ stop ] - stop speaking.
  - [ actions ]
     - save text to pasteboard.
     - setting speech voice and volumes.
     - setting recognition languages.
     - setting recognition options.
     - Delete the image.
* Touching the image that toggles to show/hide the buttons

iOS 16 supports English (United States), French (France), Italian (Italy), German (Germany),
Spanish (Spain), Portuguese (Brazil),
Chinese Simplified, Chinese Traditional, Cantonese Simplified, Cantonese Traditional,
Korean (South Korea), Japanese (Japan), Russian (Russia), Ukrainian (Ukraine)

iOS 17 supports more languages like Thai (Thailand), vi-VT.
